SMRS Cancer Center

Cancer Centre at Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences is one of the most advanced and comprehensive Cancer Care Centres in India. Our most advanced cancer institute is equipped with state of the art modern Cancer Diagnostic and Treatment facilities. The KIMS Cancer Centre is a venture with an investment of over Rs.100 Crores; a venture driven by passion for quality patient care; a venture driven by desire to make international standards of cancer care available to all sections of people in India and also abroad.

Comprehensive Cancer Diagnosis at the hospital has the most revolutionary and advanced Radiation Diagnostic and Therapeutic facilities at the full fledged Radiation Oncology Department. By installing the world’s most advanced facilities - India’s first High Definition PET-CT Scan, Image Guided RapidArc®, Radiosurgery & Body Radiosurgery in our state of the art Cancer Centre we seek to revolutionize Cancer Care in the Country.
