Accident and Emergency Medical Services Department

About the Department:

The Accident & Emergency Medical Services Department is well equipped and is managed by qualified emergency physicians. The department has an easy access for the patients coming in ambulance. The Radiology Department is closely located to the Accident & Medical Services Department to carry out immediate Radiological Interventions.

All kinds of Accidents, Trauma, & Acute care presentations are managed effectively by practicing benchmark clinical protocols and clinical processes.

Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences has a unique specialized trauma team composed of Trauma surgeons, Trauma fellows, Surgical Residents, Anesthesiologists, Emergency Medical Physicians and Technicians for managing Trauma cases. A dedicated Trauma switchboard is alerted to attend the Poly Trauma victim immediately.

SMRS- Emergency Medical Services(EMS)

Emergency Medical Services at Krishna Institute of Medical Sciences has fully equipped ambulances managed by qualified Emergency Medical Technicians for transporting victims with appropriate pre-hospital care. The Inter Facility Critical Care Transports requiring ventilator support are transported by Anesthesiologists.

The services are directly accessible through KIMS emergency contact number – 040-27844444, 040-44885555, 9849990401
