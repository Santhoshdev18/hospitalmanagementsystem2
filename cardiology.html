About The Department

The Department of Cardiology at SMR Institute of Medical Sciences

  • Round the clock comprehensive Cardiology Services

  • Emergency primary Angioplasty

  • Cardio Vascular invasive and non-invasive Diagnostics and Interventions

Expertise

The Department is supervised by six full time Senior Consultant Cardiologists and a team of full time Residents, Postgraduate Fellows and a fully fledged team of Cardiovascular Technologists.

We are geared to handle even the most complex cases, with every process structured to ensure that patient care and comfort takes precedence over everything else.
